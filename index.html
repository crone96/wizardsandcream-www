Wizards & Cream

 

Nearby an unremarkable village, a lone rogue once discovered an ancient Amulet.
 With its immeasurable power, the world could have become a better place.
Unfortunately, the Amulet was taken by a cult of weirdos...

  • Over 40 different monsters.
  • 26 challenging levels.
  • Proceduraly generated maps, using an advanced world-builder.
  • 64-bit graphics.
  • Inspired by 1980's sword & sorcery computer games.

 

In development for Windows, Linux, and Apple computers.
